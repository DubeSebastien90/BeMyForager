# Privacy Policy for BeMyForager

**Last updated:** June 18, 2026

BeMyForager ("the App", "we", "our") is a plant identification app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information Stored Only on Your Device
The following data is stored locally on your device and is **never transmitted to or stored on our servers**:

- **Plant photos** you capture or import to identify plants
- **GPS location** of each plant sighting, including a human-readable place name (e.g. city or landmark), generated on your device using Apple's or Google's built-in location services
- **Timestamps** of each plant sighting
- **App language preference**

You can delete this data at any time by deleting individual sightings in the App or by uninstalling the App.

### Information Sent to Our Servers
To identify a plant, the photo you capture is sent to our backend (hosted on Supabase), which forwards it to third-party plant identification and plant-data services (PlantNet, Trefle, GBIF) for processing. These photos are used only to return identification results and are **not retained** by our backend after processing.

### Analytics and Crash Data
We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage and diagnose technical issues. This includes:

- App events: when a plant is identified, when a plant is saved, when the app language is changed, and when the demo content is loaded
- Standard device information automatically collected by Firebase: app version, operating system version, device model, and a Firebase installation identifier
- Crash reports, including device model, OS version, and technical error logs

This data is **not linked to your identity** and is **not used for advertising**. We do not use an advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID), and the App does not show ads.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not require or support account creation, sign-in, or sign-up
- We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
- We do not collect or transmit your precise location to any server — location is processed entirely on your device
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

## 3. How We Use Information

- To identify plants from photos you submit
- To improve app stability and performance (crash reports)
- To understand general usage patterns (analytics events)

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

- **PlantNet** – plant identification
- **Trefle** – plant trait data
- **GBIF** – species taxonomy data
- **Supabase** – secure backend relay for the above services
- **Firebase (Google)** – analytics and crash reporting
- **Apple / Google location services** – on-device location-to-place-name conversion

## 5. Data Retention

Locally stored data (photos, sightings, location names) remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. We do not retain copies of your photos on our servers.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Your Choices

- You may delete any saved plant sighting within the App at any time
- You may uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
- You may disable location permissions in your device settings; the App will continue to function without location-based features

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this same URL with an updated revision date.
